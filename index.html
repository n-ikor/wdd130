Delta, Nigeria

This image represents my state of origin, Delta State, Nigeria, a vibrant region rich in cultural heritage and natural beauty. Delta is known for a lot of things, including its lush landscapes, diverse ethnic groups such as the Urhobo, Isoko, Ndokwa, Itsekiri, and Ijaw, and a thriving oil industry that plays a significant role in Nigeria's economy. However, one standout feature is the majestic River Niger, which flows through the capital city, Asaba, bringing life to the region with its scenic views and economic activities. The river continues its journey southward, eventually emptying into the Gulf of Guinea, shaping the state's geography and supporting its fishing and trade communities along the way.
